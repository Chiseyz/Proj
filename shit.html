‚Ä¢ Use active recall: test yourself regularly without looking at notes

Campus Facilities Guide

Know where everything is and how to use campus facilities.

University Library

Location: Main Building, 2nd Floor

Description: The UC Library offers a comprehensive collection of books, journals, research materials, and digital resources. Study areas include individual desks, group study rooms, and computer workstations.

Hours: Monday-Friday: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM | Saturday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Rules: Maintain silence, no food or drinks except water, present ID when borrowing materials, return books on time to avoid fines.

University Clinic

Location: Ground Floor, beside Registrar's Office

Description: The clinic provides first aid, basic medical consultation, and health services. A registered nurse is on duty during school hours.

Hours: Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Services: First aid, health assessment, medical certificates, health education, emergency response.

Reminder: For serious medical emergencies, call emergency services immediately.

Canteen

Location: Ground Floor, near the quad & 7th floor near the elevator

Description: The canteen offers affordable meals, snacks, and beverages for students and faculty. Multiple food stalls provide variety.

Hours: Monday-Friday: 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Rules: Clean up after eating, dispose trash properly, respect the queue, no taking food outside without proper containers.

Computer Laboratory

Location: 3rd Floor, IT Building

Description: Equipped with computers, internet, and software for various courses. Available for research, projects, and coursework.

Hours: Monday-Friday: 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM | Saturday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Rules: No food or drinks, no downloading unauthorized software, save work frequently, log out after use, report any technical issues to the lab assistant.

Guidance Office

Location: 2nd Floor, Administration Building

Description: Provides counseling services, academic guidance, career counseling, and personal development support. Professional counselors are available for consultation.

Hours: Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Services: Individual counseling, academic advising, career planning, crisis intervention, peer counseling programs.

Note: All consultations are confidential.

Registrar's Office

Location: Ground Floor, Administration Building

Description: Handles enrollment, grades, transcripts, certifications, and academic records.

Hours: Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Services: Enrollment processing, transcript requests, certificate issuance (good moral, enrollment, grades), grade verification, student records management.

Reminder: Bring valid ID for all transactions. Processing times vary by document type.
